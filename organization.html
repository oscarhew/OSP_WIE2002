Organizations

There are organisations that work tirelessly to provide aid, resources, and education to those in need in order to combat poverty. These organisations' efforts help to improve the quality of life for millions of people around the world and provide hope for a better future.

Below are several of them:
Oxfam International
Oxfam is a group of 20 charities that want to help poor people and make things fair. They started in 1995 and are based in Kenya. Oxfam gives aid to places affected by war, disasters, and poverty. They work with locals, partners, and governments to make life better and help people escape poverty.
Read more
Organization for Poverty Alleviation and Development (OPAD)
OPAD is a group that helps poor communities get things like education, healthcare, and clean water. They work with local partners in countries like Ethiopia, Ghana, and India to create programs that tackle issues such as food, gender, environment, and climate. OPAD also prepares communities for disasters to help them become stronger. Their goal is to make a long-lasting difference in the lives of people who are living in poverty.
Read more
Concern Worldwide
Concern Worldwide is a charity that started in Ireland in 1968. They work in 23 countries to help people suffering from poverty and conflict. Concern provides health, education, and livelihood programs, and works to reduce disaster risks. They believe in empowering women and help during emergencies, like war and natural disasters. They work with local partners to make sure their programs work well and last long.
Read more
CARE International
CARE International is a group of charities that fight poverty and promote fairness. They started in 1945 and work in over 100 countries. CARE focuses on empowering women and girls and helping everyone get education, healthcare, and food. They also help during emergencies. CARE wants to help people escape poverty and make a lasting difference in their lives.
Read more
Poverty Action
Poverty Action is a charity that studies how to reduce poverty in poor countries. They work with governments and other groups to do tests and research to find the best ways to help people. Poverty Action looks at things like health, education, finance, and farming. They want to help people have a better life, no matter what challenges they face.
Read more
Advocates for International (A4ID)
A4ID is a charity that connects lawyers with people who need legal help for development, human rights, and environmental issues. They offer training and support to help lawyers become better at their work. A4ID wants to strengthen the law and make sure everyone gets justice, especially in poor countries. They aim to empower people, promote human rights, and help achieve the UN's goals for sustainable development.
